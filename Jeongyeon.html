Jeongyeon (Twice) Facts and Trivia
  •  Stage Name:  Jeongyeon

  •  Zodiac Sign:  Scorpio
  •  Official height  169 cm (5’7″) / Approx. Real Height: 167 cm (5’6″)*

  •  Jeongyeon facts: 
  • – She was born in Suwon, South Korea.
  • – Jeongyeon’s birth name is Yoo Kyung Wan. She changed her name because she was teased as her name sounded like a boy’s name.
